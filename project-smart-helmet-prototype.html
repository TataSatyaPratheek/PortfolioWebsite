Smart Helmet Prototype

Description: Developed a wearable device to monitor and visualize athletic performance, specifically focusing on boxing punch analysis.

Skills: Sensor Integration, BLE Data Transmission, Data Visualization

Project Details

By integrating advanced sensors and BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) technology, the Smart Helmet Prototype was able to capture real-time data on punch force and location. This data was then visualized to provide athletes with actionable insights to improve their performance and ensure safety during training sessions.
