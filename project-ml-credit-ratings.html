Machine Learning and Sovereign Credit Ratings

Description: Using machine learning models to forecast credit ratings, integrating economic and political factors to influence financial decisions.

Skills: Financial Modeling, AI, Statistical Analysis

Project Details

Developed advanced machine learning models to predict sovereign credit ratings by integrating a variety of economic indicators and political factors. The project involved extensive data preprocessing, feature engineering, and model optimization to achieve high predictive accuracy. This tool aids financial institutions in making informed investment decisions and managing risk effectively.
